Project Overview

Problem

Bangla crime news articles are often long, repetitive, and difficult to skim, especially when reading multiple reports. Manual summarization is time-consuming.

Approach

We fine-tune BanglaT5 and mT5 on a curated Bangla crime dataset, generate multiple candidate summaries, then use a suite of metrics (SBERT, ROUGE, BLEU, CER, coverage, length, etc.) to automatically select the best candidate.

Deliverables

A working web interface, dataset analysis dashboard, detailed quantitative metrics, and a defense report describing the full research pipeline end-to-end.
